Philippine Military Academy

Bagiuo City

#1 The Philippine Military Academy (PMA) is the premier military institution in the Philippines. Located in Baguio City, it is where young men and women undergo rigorous training to become officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines.

Read More

Puzzle Mansion

Tagaytay City

#2 Puzzle Mansion in Tagaytay City is home to a vast collection of jigsaw puzzles, making it the largest collection in the world according to Guinness World Records.

Read More

Igorot Stone Kingdom

Bagiuo City

#3 The Igorot Stone Kingdom in Baguio City is a unique attraction that features stone sculptures and carvings created by local Igorot artists. The stone artworks depict traditional Igorot culture, folklore, and symbols, showcasing the rich heritage and artistic skills of the indigenous people of the Cordillera region.

Read More

Mines View Park

Bagiuo City

#4 Mine View Park in Baguio City offers visitors a glimpse into the region's mining history and scenic views of the surrounding mountains. The park features a viewing deck where visitors can enjoy panoramic vistas of the city and nearby mines.

Read More

Burnham Park

Bagiuo City

#5 Burnham Park is a popular urban park located at the heart of Baguio City. Named after American architect Daniel Burnham, who designed the city's master plan, the park offers a variety of recreational activities for visitors.

Read More

Chinese Bell Church

Baguio City

#6 The Chinese Bell Church in Baguio City is a serene place of worship and cultural center for the local Chinese community. It showcases traditional Chinese architecture, intricate ornaments, and vibrant colors.

Read More

Strawbery Farm

Bagiuo City

#7 The Strawberry Farm is a popular tourist destination in Baguio City, Philippines. Located in the neighboring municipality of La Trinidad, it offers visitors the opportunity to experience strawberry picking, enjoy fresh strawberries, and immerse themselves in the beautiful agricultural landscapes.

Read More

SBMA Base Tour

Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority

#8 SBMA, located in the province of Zambales, is a renowned tourist destination known for its natural beauty and historical significance. It was formerly a United States naval base but has since been transformed into a freeport zone and a thriving hub for tourism, commerce, and leisure activities.
