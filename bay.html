Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority

Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority

Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority is located in the province of Zambales, is a renowned tourist destination known for its natural beauty and historical significance. It was formerly a United States naval base but has since been transformed into a freeport zone and a thriving hub for tourism, commerce, and leisure activities.

To go to the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority, you can follow these general directions:

  1. By Air:
    1. Fly to Clark International Airport: If you're coming from outside the Philippines or from other parts of the country, you can book a flight to Clark International Airport (CRK) in Pampanga, which is the nearest major airport to Subic Bay. Several airlines operate domestic and international flights to Clark.
    2. From Clark to Subic Bay: Upon arrival at Clark International Airport, you have a few options to get to Subic Bay: Private Transfer: You can hire a private car or arrange for a transfer service from the airport to Subic Bay. Many hotels and resorts offer this service. Shuttle Service: Some travel agencies and shuttle companies provide shared transfers from Clark to Subic Bay. You can check for availability and schedules in advance. Public Transportation: From the airport, you can take a jeepney or taxi to the Dau Bus Terminal, and from there, catch a bus bound for Olongapo City. From Olongapo, you can take a local jeepney or taxi to Subic Bay.

Igorot Stone Kingdom

Bagiuo City

#3 The Igorot Stone Kingdom in Baguio City is a unique attraction that features stone sculptures and carvings created by local Igorot artists. The stone artworks depict traditional Igorot culture, folklore, and symbols, showcasing the rich heritage and artistic skills of the indigenous people of the Cordillera region.
