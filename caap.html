CIVIL AVIATION AUTHORITY
OF PHILIPPINES

Speaker
Mr. Louie Diawan

The Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines is the government agency responsible for regulating and overseeing civil aviation operations in the Philippines. The CAAP ensures the safety, security, and efficiency of the country's aviation industry.

One topic during the session was about Detecting Aircraft using the satellite. They can detect an aircraft's presence, position, and altitude using the raday and the object is above the radar. This enables the CAAP to track and identify aircraft in real-time.

As a Computer Science student, the visit to the Civil Aviation of the Philippies and the knowledge gained from the discussions offer one insight to me:

First is Information Management, computer science plays a critical role in managing the vast amounts of information that the CAAP deals with on a daily basis. This includes aircraft registration data, flight plans, maintenance records, safety regulations, and more. Computer scientists develop systems and algorithms to store, retrieve, and process this information efficiently, ensuring its accuracy and availability when needed.
