Burnham Park

Burnham Park

Burnham Park is a popular urban park located in Baguio City, Philippines. It is named after the American architect and urban planner, Daniel Hudson Burnham, who was commissioned to design Baguio City in the early 1900s. The park covers a sprawling area of approximately 32.84 hectares and serves as a central recreational hub for both locals and tourists.

Here's an overview of what Burnham Park offers:

  1. Burnham Lake: One of the main attractions within Burnham Park is the man-made lake. Visitors can rent colorful pedal boats shaped like swans or ducks and enjoy a leisurely ride on the serene waters. The lake is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a picturesque spot for relaxation and photo opportunities.

To go to the Philippine Military Academy (PMA) in Baguio City, you can follow these general directions:

  1. By Air: If you're traveling from outside of Baguio City, you can book a flight to Manila, which is the capital city of the Philippines. From Manila, you have several options to reach Baguio City:
    1. By Bus: Go to any of the major bus terminals in Manila (e.g., Victory Liner, Genesis Transport) and look for a bus bound for Baguio City. Travel time is approximately 4-6 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.
    2. By Private Vehicle: If you have your own car or are planning to rent one, you can take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and exit at the SCTEX (Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway) in Tarlac. From there, take the MacArthur Highway until you reach Baguio City. Travel time is approximately 4-5 hours, depending on traffic.

  2. By Land: If you are already in the Philippines and prefer to travel by land, you have a few options:
    1. From Manila: You can take a bus from any of the major bus terminals in Manila to Baguio City. Follow the instructions provided in Step 1a above.
    2. From other parts of Luzon: If you are coming from another part of Luzon, you can take a bus or drive to Baguio City using the major highways that connect to it.

  3. Arrival in Baguio City: Once you arrive in Baguio City, you can hire a taxi or use a ridesharing app like Grab to reach the Burnham Park. You can simply tell the driver to take you to the Burnham Park, and they should be familiar with the location.
