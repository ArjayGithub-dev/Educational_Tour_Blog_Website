HYTEC POWER, INCORPORATED

Speaker
Ms. Jessica

Hytec Power, Incorporated is an innovative company providing products and services for both academe and industry.
They are consistently providing industrial solutions to common problems in the industry to achieve work productivity, operational efficiency and workplace safety. They offer solutions to job skills mismatch in the country. They supply not only equipment and services to academe and industry but also provide rigorous after sales training and support.

Ms. Jessica introduced us their robot named Pepper, where it follows what the user wants and can answer any questions. I was really amazed that this robot knows how to dance.

Apart from Pepper, HYTEC has a lot to offer, from robots and machines to software and hardware. Isn't it incredible? They are indeed hightech.

This company is the closest one that aligns well with my background in computer science and has a strong connection to my interests. First, robots are not only compose of hardware, but it is run by algorithm, which is basically one of the fundamental of computer science. Artificial intelligence is also applied to Pepper for him to intereact with the user.

Aside from Ms. Jessica there are other speaker who shared their expertise and introduce us with some of their inventions.
