Puzzle Mansion

Puzzle Mansion

This tourist attraction is by far one of my favorite place I visited during the tour. The Puzzle Mansion located at Tagaytay City, from the name itself, you will expect a bunch of puzzles inside of the mansion, but it does not only stop to an ordinary puzzle that most of us used to play. Mr. Mark Adrian Balinao shared to us the story of Puzzle Mansion, Gina Lacuna, a retired businesswoman, developed a deep love for puzzles in the early 1980s. What began as a hobby quickly turned into an obsession as she started collecting jigsaw puzzles of various sizes, themes, and complexities.

Her collection grew steadily over the years, fueled by her enthusiasm and the joy she found in solving and assembling puzzles. Gina's dedication to her puzzle collection caught the attention of the Guinness World Records organization. In 2012, after rigorous verification, her collection was officially recognized as the largest collection of jigsaw puzzles in the world. This achievement brought widespread recognition to Gina and her remarkable collection. Motivated by the desire to share her passion with others, Gina decided to transform her family's vacation house in Tagaytay City into a museum. The Puzzle Mansion Museum was born, becoming the new home for her extensive puzzle collection.

To go to Puzzle Mansion in Tagaytay City, you can follow these general directions:

  1. By Public Transportation:
    1. From Manila: You can take a bus bound for Tagaytay City from any of the major bus terminals in Manila (e.g., Buendia, Cubao). Look for buses with signs indicating "Tagaytay" or "Nasugbu." Inform the driver or conductor that you're heading to Puzzle Mansion. The bus ride takes approximately 1.5 to 2.5 hours, depending on traffic conditions.
    2. From Tagaytay City: Upon reaching Tagaytay City, you can hire a tricycle or take a jeepney heading to Barangay Asisan. Inform the driver that you want to go to Puzzle Mansion. The ride should take about 15 minutes.

  2. By Private Vehicle:
    1. From Manila: Take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and exit at Santa Rosa or Silang. Follow the signs leading to Tagaytay City. Once in Tagaytay, drive towards the Aguinaldo Highway, which is the main road. Continue driving until you reach Barangay Asisan. Puzzle Mansion is located along the highway, and there are signs indicating its presence.

  3. Arrival at Puzzle Mansion: Upon arrival, you'll find the Puzzle Mansion easily recognizable by its distinctive architecture. It's a large mansion painted in vibrant colors. The staff will be happy to assist you upon entry.
