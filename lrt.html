LIGHT RAIL TRANSIT AUTHORITY -
LINE 2

Speaker
Mr. Jesson Tamondong

The Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA) is a government agency in the Philippines responsible for the operation, maintenance, and development of the light rail transit (LRT) system in Metro Manila. Mr. Jesson first presented us a video about the history and things to know about the LRT.

LRT Line 2, also known as the Purple Line or the Megatren, is the second rapid transit line in Metro Manila. Its construction began in the early 1990s, and the first segment, which runs from Recto Avenue in Manila to Santolan in Pasig, was inaugurated on April 5, 2003.

Well, computer science plays a vital role in designing and implementing ticketing and payment systems for public transportation. The LRTA may rely on computerized systems that handle ticketing, fare collection, and data management. This involves the use of software, databases, and secure payment processing algorithms.
